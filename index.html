Why Sketchlang?

Write once, draw anywhere

With Sketchlang, you can draw anything to your heart's desire, without thinking about the platform. Eliminate the need of "this drew on my machine" while keeping your satisfaction at its finest.

Prefer the command line version?

No fret! Sketchlang is also a command-line drawing interpreter, which is mostly compatible with full Sketchlang code, and have your graphics be shown at a window!
